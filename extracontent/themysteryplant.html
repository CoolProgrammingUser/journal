The Mystery Plant

This is a series I wrote in fourth grade when we had the chance to free write. To help you know my writing level at this time, I've copied it without correcting any spelling or grammar errors. It's worth noting, though, that a few of the mistakes may have been one-time errors. Also, I improved my writing as the story progressed. The story is told primarily as a dialogue between characters despite the conspicuous lack of quotation marks. Although the very beginning mentions three main characters (which was a surprise to me), I quickly forget about the third person and center the story around two characters and occasionally a mayor.
