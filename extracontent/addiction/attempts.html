Attempts to quit

This is a list of tactics I've used to try to overcome my addiction.

  • Begging myself
  • Reminding myself of my motivations
  • Pinching myself (to associate it with pain)
  • Overindulging (to make myself sick of it)
  • Giving it up for Lent (I have no idea why this was successful at all.)
  • Only touching in sanctioned ways
  • Leveraging my loss of p03
  • (Inadvertently) burying myself in schoolwork
  • Adopting a certain mindset:
    • Just stop.
    • You're better than this.
    • This is too painful.
    • This is an addiction.
    • Don't think about it.
    • It doesn't matter whether you think this should or shouldn't be okay.
    • You're incapable of making a good decision in the moment.
    • This is destroying your life.
